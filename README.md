# 🏦 Banking Customer Risk Analysis Dashboard

A comprehensive **Banking Customer Risk Analysis** project built using **Python, SQL, and Power BI** to analyze customer risk profiles, financial exposure, and customer segmentation. This project helps banks identify high-risk customers, improve lending decisions, and enhance customer retention through data-driven insights.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

Banks handle large volumes of customer data, making it difficult to identify high-risk customers using traditional methods. This project analyzes customer demographics, financial behavior, income levels, deposits, loans, and loyalty classifications to provide actionable insights for effective risk management.

The project combines **data cleaning**, **exploratory data analysis (EDA)**, **SQL querying**, and an **interactive Power BI dashboard** to support business decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Financial institutions need to:

* Identify customers with high credit risk.
* Reduce loan defaults.
* Understand factors influencing customer risk.
* Improve lending strategies.
* Increase customer retention.
* Support management with real-time business insights.

# 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer risk distribution.

# 📊 Key Insights

* Risk Level 2 contains the largest number of customers.
* Medium-income customers represent the majority of the customer base.
* European customers form the largest customer segment.
* Jade is the most common loyalty classification.
* Platinum customers generally exhibit lower risk profiles.
* Higher deposits are associated with stronger financial stability.
* Income band is a key factor influencing customer risk.
